I do not know the number

The number should be in your letter. It is in the second of the four ‘How to renew online' steps.

If you no longer have the letter try searching your email for the renewal email, which should have a link directly to your renewal online.

If you cannot find either the letter or the email, call the Environment Agency helpline on 03708 506506 (Monday to Friday, 8am to 6pm) or email nccc-carrierbroker@environment-agency.gov.uk.
