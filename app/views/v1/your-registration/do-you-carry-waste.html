What is a carrier, broker or dealer?

Your carrier type can include any or all of the below:

  • carriers transport commercial, industrial and household waste, known as controlled waste, as part of their business
  • brokers arrange for other businesses' controlled waste to be handled, transported, disposed of or recovered
  • dealers buy and sell waste, either for their own business or on behalf of another organisation

You should select the option that covers all of your activities (for example, carrier-dealer or carrier-broker-dealer) even if you rarely do some of them (for example, you are usually a carrier but occasionally arrange for someone else to carry waste for you).
