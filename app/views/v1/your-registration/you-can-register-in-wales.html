You can register in Wales

You can continue to renew in England, but you’re encouraged to register with the appropriate authority for your region. This allows customers to verify that you are a registered waste carrier.

In Wales you need to register with Natural Resources Wales (NRW). For details on how, visit Register or renew as a waste carrier, broker or dealer (Wales).

If you have any questions about this, please contact our helpline on 03708 506 506.
